Move - Arrow Keys
Powers - 1-5
Start - Space

The aim is to loose all your coins!

Powers:
- Gravity: Forces you to go down more
- Anti-Coins: Coins are worth negative
- Freeplay: Disables auto-slide
- Clone: Create a duplicate and split the coins
- Combust: Explode your current player and loose all current coins

Gravity, Anti-Coins and Freeplay last 30 seconds each.
